Songfu Cai

 

Post-doctoral Fellow,
Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering,
The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology

I received my Ph.D. in Electronic and Computer Engineering from HKUST in Jul. 2019, under the supervision of Prof. Vincent K. N. Lau . I was a postdoctoral scholar at HKUST, working with Prof. Vincent K. N. Lau in 2019-22. Since Jan. 2023, I have been a postdoc in Prof. Xuanyu Cao's research group at HKUST, where I have a great time exploring multidisciplinary research on optimization, AI and control theory. I will be joining the College of Information Science & Electronic Engineering at Zhejiang University as a ZJU-100 Young Professor in Jan. 2025.

I am broadly interested in learning theory, optimization, dynamic games and networked control.
